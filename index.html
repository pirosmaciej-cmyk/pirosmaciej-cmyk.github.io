  • Design modifications and updates

Benefits:

3D Scanning

High-precision 3D scanning technology to capture and digitize physical objects.

What We Offer:

  • Laser and structured light scanning
  • Industrial-grade accuracy (±0.1mm)
  • Point cloud generation and processing
  • Surface reconstruction and mesh generation

Benefits:

Capture reality with precision. Perfect for documentation, quality control, and digitizing existing components for reproduction or modification.

Reverse Engineering

Convert existing products into detailed CAD models through advanced scanning and analysis.

What We Offer:

  • 3D to CAD conversion
  • Complex component analysis
  • Part reproduction documentation
  • Design improvement suggestions

Benefits:

Unlock legacy designs. Whether for preservation, modification, or reproduction, we convert physical products into editable digital models.

  • Material selection consultation
  • Rapid prototyping services
  • Post-processing and finishing

Benefits:

From concept to physical reality. Rapidly prototype and iterate designs, test functionality, and produce end-use parts with precision.
